# Tinder for Dogs — Account Management

## Creating a profile
Each account can have up to 3 dog profiles. Each profile requires:
- At least 1 photo (max 10 MB, JPG or PNG)
- Dog's name, breed, age, and size (small / medium / large)
- Energy level (low / medium / high)

## Editing a profile
Go to Settings > My Dogs > select the dog > Edit Profile.
Changes are reflected in the feed within 15 minutes.

## Deleting a profile
Settings > My Dogs > select the dog > Delete Profile > Confirm.
This action is irreversible. All matches and chat history for that dog are permanently deleted.
Deleting all dog profiles does not delete the account itself.

## Deleting the account
Settings > Account > Delete Account > Confirm with password.
All data is deleted within 30 days per GDPR Article 17.

## Subscription and billing
Subscriptions are managed via the App Store or Google Play.
Tinder for Dogs cannot issue refunds directly — contact Apple or Google support.
Paw Premium renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.
