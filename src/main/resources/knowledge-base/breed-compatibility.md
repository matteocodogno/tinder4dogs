# Breed Compatibility Guide

## Golden Retriever
Golden Retrievers are sociable and gentle. They pair well with Labradors, Beagles,
and Poodles due to similar energy levels and playful temperament.
They may overwhelm shy or small breeds like Chihuahuas or Shih Tzus.
Compatibility warning: strong prey drive around toy breeds.

## Labrador Retriever
Labradors are high-energy and social. Excellent matches: Golden Retrievers, Boxers,
Border Collies. They tend to be too boisterous for elderly or low-energy breeds.
Labradors are generally good with other dogs when socialised early.

## French Bulldog
French Bulldogs have low-to-medium energy and prefer calm companions.
Best matches: Cavalier King Charles Spaniels, Pugs, Shih Tzus.
They can struggle with high-energy breeds like Border Collies or Huskies.
Heat sensitivity means outdoor playdates should be short in summer.

## Border Collie
Border Collies are extremely high-energy and intelligent. They need equally active partners.
Best matches: Australian Shepherds, Huskies, Labradors.
Avoid matching with low-energy or elderly dogs — Border Collies may herd them,
causing stress. Not recommended for first-time dog owners' matches.

## Chihuahua
Chihuahuas are small and can be territorial. Best matches: other Chihuahuas, Pomeranians,
Dachshunds. Matching with large breeds is risky unless the large dog has a known
gentle temperament. Chihuahuas may feel threatened and react defensively.

## Husky
Huskies are high-energy with a strong pack instinct. Great matches: Malamutes,
Border Collies, Labradors. They have a high prey drive and should not be matched
with very small breeds. Huskies thrive with companions who can keep up on long runs.
