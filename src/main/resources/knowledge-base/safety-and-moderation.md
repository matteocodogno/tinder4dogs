# Tinder for Dogs — Safety and Moderation

## Reporting a profile
Tap the flag icon on any dog profile card. Select a reason:
- Fake profile (not a real dog)
- Inappropriate content
- Spam or commercial account
- Suspected abuse or neglect

Reports are reviewed by the moderation team within 24 hours.
Repeated reports on a profile trigger an automatic temporary suspension pending review.

## Blocked profiles
Blocking a dog removes it from your feed and prevents any future matches.
The blocked owner is not notified. Go to Settings > Blocked Dogs to manage the list.

## Meeting safely in person
Tinder for Dogs recommends first meetings in public, dog-friendly spaces.
Always ensure your dog is on a leash for the first introduction.
Never share your home address in the chat before meeting in a neutral location.

## Content policy
Profile photos must show the dog clearly. Human faces in photos are automatically blurred.
Photos containing weapons, nudity, or drug paraphernalia are automatically rejected.
